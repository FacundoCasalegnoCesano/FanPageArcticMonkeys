Whatever People Say I Am Tour (2005–2006)

El Whatever People Say I Am Tour realizado por la banda de rock Arctic Monkeys, fue su primer tour y la presentación de su primer disco musical.

Favourite Worst Nightmare Tour (2007)

El Favourite Worst Nightmare Tour realizado por la banda de rock Arctic Monkeys, se llevo a cabo en Europa,America latina, America del norte, Asia y Oceania. En el mismo se tocaron las siguientes canciones: This House Is a Circus,Still Take You Home,Dancing Shoes,Brianstorm,From the Ritz to the Rubble,Teddy Picker,Balaclava,Fake Tales of San Francisco,I Bet You Look Good on the Dancefloor,D Is for Dangerous,Old Yellow Bricks,The View From the Afternoon,Fluorescent Adolescent,Do Me a Favour,When the Sun Goes Down,If You Were There, Beware,Leave Before the Lights Come On,A Certain Romance

Humbug Tour (2009–2010)

El Humbug Tour realizado por la banda de rock Arctic Monkeys se llevo a cabo en America del Norte, Europa y Oceania. AM Tour (2013–2014)

El AM Tour se llevo a por todo el mundo y fue el tour mas exitoso de la banda hasta la fecha. Se tocaron las siguientes pistas: "Do I Wanna Know?", R U Mine?, Dancing Shoes, Brianstorm, Brick by Brick, Don't Sit Down 'Cause I've Moved Your Chair, Evil Twin, Old Yellow Bricks, The Hellcat Spangled Shalalala, Crying Lightning, Pretty Visitors, Do Me a Favour, Cornerstone, She's Thunderstorms, The View from the Afternoon, Fake Tales of San Francisco, I Bet You Look Good on the Dancefloor, That's Where You're Wrong.

Tranquility Base Hotel & Casino Tour (2018-2019)

El Tranquility Base Hotel & Casino Tour se llevo a cabo por Europa, America del Norte, Oceania y America del Sur. El setlist fue el siguiente:"Four Out of Five", Brianstorm, Don't Sit Down 'Cause I've Moved Your Chair, Crying Lightning, Teddy Picker, Snap Out Of It, 505, Do Me A Favour, Tranquility Base Hotel & Casino, One Point Perspective, Do I Wanna Know?, Cornerstone, Why'd You Only Call Me When You're High?, Knee Socks, I Bet You Look Good on the Dancefloor, Arabella, One for the Road, Pretty Visitors.
